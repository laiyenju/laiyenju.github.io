This project is a 2-month summer internship design proposal in [Public Digital Innovation Space](https://pdis.nat.gov.tw/en/). 

- We are four-members team, grouped by designer, engineer and researcher.
- I planned the user research and analyse the result, leading team following our analytics to complete high-fidelity prototype.

| Problems | Solution |
| -------- | -------- |
| Taiwan Jobs website, operated by government, aims to provide service for unemployees. However, Ministry of Labor want to stand out from the other competitors, level up the website usage and public satisfication. | After the research and user testing, we proposed a whole design package, from positioning the service, rebuilding information architecture and website design guideline. To combine the offline service with online service in one place. |

### 3. Framing Key Problems

#### Key Problem 1: Taiwan Jobs does not have competance with other employment-oriented online service.


#### Key Problem 2: People don't know what can government provide even they visited this website.

such as

- The granularity of navigation menu is inconsistant with ambiguous label that confused users.
- Users reading behavior are being interupting by outbounced link to other website.
- Users mental workload can not afford the overwhelming information expressed in legal terminalogy.

![](https://i.imgur.com/VAocOB1.jpg)

#### Key Problem 3: Inconsistant interface design that doesn't fit users' daily experience.

such as

- The search result returns empty if user set up more than 3 filters.
- Users are lack of sense of secutity because they are not sure whether ex-employer saw the resume.

#### 4.5 validation and high-fidelity prototype

We conducted SUS evaluation and several interviews through 5 tasks.

- task 1: search for an occupation and submit resume and cover letter
- task 2: edit the education and experience after log in/ registration
- task 3: make a reservation for career consultant service in Taipei on Sep 6th 14:00
- task 4: apply for lay-off subsidy 
- task 5: make a reservation for online career consultant on Sep 6th 14:00

After the iteration between test and prototyping, we found that users especially the elder people are more familiar with using mobile phone. Therefore, we made not only desktop version but also utilise it as responsive web design for mobile user.
